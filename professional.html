Professional

I currently work at the Children's Hospital of Philadelphia as a Data Scientist in the Data Science and Biostatistics Unit . I graduated from Cornell University summa cum laude with a Bachlor of Arts in Computer Science, in May 2021. I am interested in studying the ways algorithms and automation impact people, our society, and our world, and how technology can be used to address the problems we face.
